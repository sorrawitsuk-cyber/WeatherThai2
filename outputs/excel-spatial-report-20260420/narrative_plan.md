# Cooling Shelter Dashboard Narrative

## Audience
Bangkok operations and planning stakeholders who need a quick view of cooling shelter coverage.

## Objective
Summarize cooling shelter distribution, coordinate quality, and spatial clustering from the March 31, 2026 dataset.

## Slide Flow
1. Title and context
2. KPI summary and key insights
3. District distribution chart
4. Subdistrict coverage and data quality
5. Spatial map from GeoJSON points

## Key Messages
- The dataset contains 599 locations with 98.83% coordinate coverage.
- เขตคลองสามวา has the highest number of locations at 58.
- Spatial coverage spans 51 districts with 183 identified subdistrict names.
